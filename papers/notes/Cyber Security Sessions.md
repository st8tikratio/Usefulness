# Cyber Security Sessions

### Cyber Tech & Risk - Phishing Protection
Date/Time: 18 October 2022 @ 2200 UTC
Host: CyberTech Risk
Speakers: Oren Falkowitz (Cloudflare), Eric Ivankovich (Microsoft Canda)
[Cyber Tech Risk Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/c/CyberTechRisk/videos)


#### Cyber Tech and Risk: Phishing Protection
- Takes little effort to spin up website, 365 account, or similar
    - send out 10 emails to a company, 95% chance someone will get 'hooked'
- Phishing is about **authenticity**
    - we want to trust (human nature)
        - brands
            - subverting or mimicing
        - themes that map to world events
            - elections
            - UN, Nato
        - internal communications
    - people study context
        - headers
        - footers
        - wire where
        - what is the scam trying to present

#### BEC [Business Environment Compromise] Attacks
![](https://i.imgur.com/jkX1cTN.jpg)
- Type 1 - BEC attack
    - looks like it's coming from the CEO, internal
    - 50/50 effectiveness
- Type 2 - Lost control of account within network
- Type 3 - partner has account breach
    - interceding in business activities
- Example
    - ![](https://i.imgur.com/45BvfWA.png)
    - business partner had account takeover
- How much of an attack can an organization handle?

#### Multi-mode Attack
- ![](https://i.imgur.com/7VA4CMI.png)
    - looks to be from business partner Octa
- ![](https://i.imgur.com/JdptP97.jpg)
    - threat actor starts to record credentials
        - usernames
        - logins
    - thwarted by Yubi keys and other 2FA authenticators

#### Environment
- Establish a paranoid and blame free culture
- Adopt comprhensive phishing schemes
- Don't be basic
    - What can we do to improve
        - security keys
        - cloud-based tools
    - Assess - take one step forward
